PQRS_0006


Measure #6 (NQF 0067): Coronary Artery Disease (CAD): Antiplatelet Therapy 
National Quality Strategy Domain: Effective Clinical Care

2015 PQRS OPTIONS FOR INDIVIDUAL MEASURES: REGISTRY ONLY


DESCRIPTION:
Percentage of patients aged 18 years and older with a diagnosis of coronary artery disease (CAD) seen within a 12 month period who were prescribed aspirin or clopidogrel


INSTRUCTIONS:
This measure is to be reported a minimum of once per reporting period for all patients with CAD seen during the reporting period. This measure may be reported by clinicians who perform the quality actions described in the measure for the primary management of patients with CAD based on the services provided and the measure-specific denominator coding.
